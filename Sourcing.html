Sourcing Candidates

Find and manage potential candidates for your job openings.

Integration with LinkedIn and Job Boards

Connect and source candidates from various platforms to expand your reach and find the best talent.

  • Direct Posting: Publish job openings created in this system directly to LinkedIn, Indeed, and other integrated job boards. This eliminates the need to manually create the same posting on multiple platforms.
  • Candidate Search: Utilize integrated search functionality to browse potential candidates on LinkedIn based on keywords, skills, location, and other criteria.
  • Profile Import: Easily import candidate profiles from LinkedIn directly into your talent pool database with a single click.
  • Job Board Aggregation (Future): In the future, this section could be expanded to aggregate candidate data from various job boards based on defined search parameters.

Talent Pool Database

Manage a centralized database of potential candidates for current and future opportunities. Build a valuable resource of talent over time.

  • Centralized Storage: Store candidate profiles, contact information, resumes, and notes in one secure and easily accessible location.
  • Tagging and Categorization: Organize candidates using tags (e.g., skills, experience level, potential roles) for efficient filtering and retrieval.
  • Search and Filtering: Quickly find relevant candidates within the talent pool based on various criteria.
  • Communication History: Track all interactions with candidates, ensuring a consistent and personalized approach.
  • Pipeline Management: Move candidates through different stages of your sourcing pipeline (e.g., identified, contacted, interested).

Resume Parser and Filter

Automate the process of extracting key information from resumes and efficiently filter candidates based on specific requirements.

  • Automatic Data Extraction: The system will automatically parse resumes uploaded by candidates or imported from external sources, extracting information such as name, contact details, education, work experience, skills, and more.
  • Customizable Parsing Rules (Future): Future enhancements could allow administrators to define custom rules for parsing specific information relevant to their industry or roles.
  • Advanced Filtering: Filter candidates based on keywords, specific skills, years of experience, education level, job titles, companies, and other criteria.
  • Ranking and Scoring (Future): Implement algorithms to rank candidates based on how well their skills and experience match the requirements of a specific job posting.
